These meters indicate how Mochiko is doing.


Love: Show your friend some love by completing coding lessons with them.

Hunger: Feed your friend with food from the shop.

Sleep: Rest is a must for you and your friend! Be sure to let them sleep when you're not spending time with them.

Money: Earn money for food, accessories, and furniture by completing coding lessons.

Be careful! If the meters get too low, Mochiko will run away. To. get them back, you'd need to complete extra coding challenges.
